About KCC

A professionally managed civil engineering and construction company serving Jammu & Kashmir with residential, commercial, and government infrastructure projects.

Kounsar Construction Company (KCC) was established to provide reliable, high-quality construction services across Jammu & Kashmir — combining qualified engineering professionals, modern machinery, and structured project management.

We undertake projects for both government and private clients, from individual homes to public infrastructure, always executed to engineering standards and agreed timelines.

Our goal is simple: build a reputation as a dependable contractor through safety, quality workmanship, and honest communication at every stage.
